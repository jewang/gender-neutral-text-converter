Gender Neutral Text Converter

Performs a first-order conversion from gendered pronouns to gender neutral: he/she → they. (Example input)
Make sure to proofread converted text!

Runs in 100% client-side Javascript. Made by Jennifer Wang. Find us on github.
Limitations
  • Cannot distinguish converting her to their vs them (her apple, say hello to her).
  • Misses some irregular verbs.
  • Misses verbs that do not immediately follow subject (e.g. she really likes hot sauce. and She dances and prances.)
